Common Questions

How are donations distributed?

Donations are distributed based on verified needs through our network of local partners, prioritizing areas with the most urgent needs.

Can I specify where my donation goes?

Yes! You can choose to support a specific request or let us allocate your donation to where it's needed most.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Crisis Connect is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How quickly are requests fulfilled?

Emergency needs are typically fulfilled within 24-48 hours, while other requests may take longer depending on complexity.
